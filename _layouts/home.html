Stefano Sarao Mannelli

I am an incoming Assistant Professor in the Data Science and AI Division in the Computer Science department of Chalmers University of Technology, currently serving as Senior Research Fellow in Gatsby & SWC at UCL. My research interests lie in building a fundamental understanding of learning in ML systems, with a particular focus on bias generation and amplification.

  • Email me
  • Twitter
  • Google Scholar

Research Directions

  • Bias generation and amplification in ML systems. What is behind biases in an ML system? What is the impact of our design choices?
  • Learning differences in biological and artificial neural networks. Curriculum learning, continual learning, transfer learning, same concept but completely different results in animals and machines, why?
  • Optimisation in rough landscapes. Connecting dynamics and landscape properties in optimisation.

Keen to know more? Check our research page or you can read our most recent publications.
